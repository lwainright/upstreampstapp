Resources

Responder & veteran support you can trust

Immediate Support

These options are available 24/7. You won’t be judged, and you won’t be reported.

  • 988 Lifeline: Call or text 988
  • 988 Veterans Line: Press 1 after calling 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

Responder Support

Confidential support from people who understand the job and the culture.

  • CopLine: 1‑800‑267‑5463 (Law Enforcement)
  • Fire/EMS Helpline: 1‑888‑731‑3473
  • Safe Call Now: 1‑206‑459‑3020 (All responders)

Veteran Support

For responders who also carry military experience.

  • Veterans Crisis Line: Call 988, then press 1
  • Vet Center: Free counseling for combat veterans
  • VA Caregiver Support: 1‑855‑260‑3274
